# TRY Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [INSERT DATE BEFORE PUBLISHING]

**This is a working draft. Have a Nigerian lawyer review it against the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 before publishing or submitting it to the App Store / Play Store.**

TRY is operated by Novapath Technologies Limited ("Novapath," "we," "us"). This policy explains what we collect from riders and drivers using the TRY app, why, and what rights you have over it.

## 1. Who this applies to

This policy covers anyone using the TRY rider app, TRY driver app, or our website. If you are a driver, additional KYC-specific terms in Section 3 apply to you.

## 2. Information we collect

### From all users (riders and drivers)
- **Phone number** — used to create your account and verify your identity via SMS one-time passcode (OTP). We do not support email-based accounts.
- **Full name** — as you enter it at sign-up.
- **Location data** — your device's GPS location, collected:
  - **Riders:** when you request a trip, to find nearby drivers and calculate fares.
  - **Drivers:** continuously while you are online and available for trips, so we can match you with nearby riders and so the rider can see your tricycle approaching.
- **Trip history** — pickup/dropoff locations, fares, timestamps, and payment method for every trip you take or complete.
- **Wallet and payment data** — your TRY wallet balance and transaction history (top-ups, trip charges, withdrawals, commission). Payment processing itself (card/bank details) is handled by our payment processor, Monnify — we never see or store your card number or bank login credentials.

### From drivers only (KYC / onboarding)
- **National Identification Number (NIN)** — collected once at sign-up to verify your identity, as required for operating a commercial passenger service in Nigeria.
- **Profile photo** — shown to riders so they can identify you and your tricycle.
- **Tricycle (Keke) plate number and a photo of the plate** — shown to riders for the same reason, and used by our team to verify your vehicle.
- **Driver's license number.**
- **Government levy receipt** (where applicable) — informational only; TRY does not collect or process levy payments on your behalf.
- **City/location at registration** — automatically detected via GPS at sign-up (not self-reported) to confirm where you operate.

We do **not** collect contacts, photos from your camera roll beyond what you explicitly choose to upload, browsing history, or data from other apps on your device.

## 3. Why we collect it

We process your data because it is necessary to:
- Create and secure your account (phone OTP).
- Match riders with nearby available drivers.
- Calculate fares and process trip payments.
- Let riders verify they're getting into the correct tricycle (driver photo, plate number).
- Verify driver identity and roadworthiness before allowing them to carry paying passengers (NIN, license, plate).
- Maintain an accurate financial ledger of wallet balances, trip earnings, and commission owed to Novapath.
- Comply with Nigerian transport and data protection regulations.
- Investigate disputes, fraud, or safety incidents.

## 4. Who we share it with

We share data only as needed to operate the service:
- **Termii** — our SMS provider, receives your phone number solely to deliver OTP codes.
- **Google Maps Platform** — receives location coordinates to calculate routes, fares, and place names. Google's own privacy policy governs how they process this.
- **Monnify** — our payment processor, receives the information needed to process wallet top-ups and driver withdrawals (e.g. amount, bank account for payout). Monnify, not TRY, handles your card/bank credentials.
- **Other users, limited to trip-matching:** when a trip is matched, the rider sees the driver's name, photo, phone number, and plate number. Drivers see the rider's pickup/dropoff location and payment method. Neither party sees the other's full account history.
- **Law enforcement or regulators**, only if legally required (e.g. a valid court order or a road-safety/transport authority investigation).

We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

## 5. Data retention

- Trip and ledger records are retained indefinitely as financial/audit records, even after an account is deleted, because Nigerian tax and commercial recordkeeping obligations require it. These records are no longer linked to your active profile once your account is deleted (see Section 6).
- Driver KYC documents (NIN, license, plate photo) are retained for as long as the driver account is active, and for a reasonable period after account closure to comply with regulatory recordkeeping.
- Location pings (live GPS while online) are not retained beyond what's needed to complete the active trip and immediate operational history.

## 6. Your rights

You can, at any time, from within the app:
- **Delete your account** — this removes your personal profile (name, phone number, photos, NIN, etc.) from our active systems. Trip and ledger records survive as anonymized financial history, as described in Section 5.
- **Request a copy of your data** or ask us to correct inaccurate data, by emailing **info@novapath.ng**.

Under the NDPA, you also have the right to object to certain processing and to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) between the app and our servers, and OTP-based authentication rather than passwords. No system is 100% secure, and we encourage you to keep your phone and SIM secure since phone number access is the primary key to your account.

## 8. Children

TRY is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated in-app before they take effect.
