## 1. What TRY is

TRY is a technology platform that connects riders who need a tricycle (Keke) trip with independent drivers willing to provide one, in Onitsha and (as we expand) other parts of Anambra State and Nigeria. **Novapath does not own, lease, or operate any tricycles, and does not employ drivers.** Drivers are independent operators who use the TRY app to find passengers. TRY is not a transport carrier — we provide the matching, navigation, and payment technology that connects riders and drivers.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and able to form a binding contract under Nigerian law to use TRY, as either a rider or a driver.

## 3. Accounts

- Accounts are tied to a phone number, verified by SMS OTP at sign-up. You're responsible for keeping access to that phone number secure — anyone who can receive your OTP can access your account.
- One account per phone number. Don't create accounts on behalf of someone else without their knowledge.
- You can delete your account at any time from within the app. See our Privacy Policy for what happens to your data after deletion.

## 4. Using TRY as a rider

- When you request a trip, TRY broadcasts your request to nearby available drivers; the first to accept is matched with you.
- The fare shown at request time is an estimate based on distance and our published per-km rate; the final fare is confirmed by the driver at trip completion and may differ slightly (e.g. for route detours).
- You may pay by **wallet balance** (pre-funded in-app) or **cash**, where the driver allows it. Cash trips may only be accepted by drivers with a sufficient wallet balance, as our way of guaranteeing TRY's commission is covered.
- You agree to treat drivers respectfully and not to request trips you don't intend to take.

## 5. Payments and wallet

- TRY charges a **10% commission** on every completed trip, deducted from the driver's earnings (wallet trips) or from the driver's wallet balance directly (cash trips).
- Wallet top-ups and driver withdrawals are processed through our payment partner, Monnify. TRY does not store your card or bank login details.
- Wallet balances are not interest-bearing and are not a bank deposit. Funds in your TRY wallet are held to facilitate trips and payouts, not as a general-purpose savings product.
- Driver withdrawals to a bank account are typically processed promptly but are not guaranteed instantaneous, as they depend on Monnify's and the receiving bank's processing times.
- All amounts are in Nigerian Naira (₦).

## 6. Driver eligibility and conduct

To register as a driver, you must:
- Provide a valid NIN, a valid tricycle, a clear photo of the tricycle's plate number, and a valid driver's license.
- Hold any local government levies, permits, or licenses required to operate a tricycle for hire in your operating area — **TRY does not obtain, pay, or guarantee these on your behalf.**
- Be the person whose photo, name, and details appear on your TRY driver profile — these are shown to riders to confirm they're getting into the right vehicle, and providing false identity information is grounds for permanent account suspension.
- Drive safely, follow applicable traffic laws, and not discriminate against riders.

TRY reviews driver applications (KYC) before approval and may reject or suspend a driver account at our discretion, including for safety complaints, fraud, repeated cancellations, or providing false KYC information.
