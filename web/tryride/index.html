Frequently asked questions

When is TRY launching?

We're launching first in Onitsha, with plans to expand across Anambra State and then nationwide. Join the waitlist and we'll text you the moment it's live in your area.

Is it safe?

Every driver goes through identity verification (NIN, license, plate number) before they're approved to carry riders. You'll always see your driver's photo and plate before a trip starts.

How do I pay?

Fund your in-app wallet for cashless trips, or pay your driver directly in cash — whichever you prefer.

How much does TRY take from drivers?

A flat 10% commission per completed trip — no hidden charges, no surge pricing games.
